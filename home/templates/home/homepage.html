About Me

I am a freelance web developer with four years of experience in the industry and I build beautiful websites for small businesses, charities and NGOs.

I can provide you with an easy to use content management system so that you have ownership over your own content and don't need any technical assistance for website updates. Your website will be fully responsive so that the user experience is tailored to whichever device is used to access it.

I attend local meetups and conferences to stay up to date on the latest web standards and technologies so that my websites are ahead of the game. I am also co-organiser of codebar Oxford and Django Girls Oxford and I am passionate about promoting diversity in tech.

Outside of work I am a dressmaker, I read a lot of books (currently on a bit of a graphic novel binge) and I cook a mean bowl of ramen.
