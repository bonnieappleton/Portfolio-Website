I am a senior software developer at ThoughtWorks. I love working on social good projects and I am passionate about improving diversity in the tech industry. I believe that tech can be used to make the world a better place!

I am a versatile developer with a broad range of experience, having started work as a QA before moving into development. I have worked on front end and back end with a variety of languages, both object-oriented and functional. At ThoughtWorks I have learnt software development best practices such as test driven development, continuous integration, pair programming and working in an autonomous team.

Outside of work I like to create - whether it's artwork (which I share on Instagram), making my own clothes, or trying to perfect my ramen recipe. I love the outdoors and a perfect day would be sat by a campfire with a cup of tea and an absorbing novel.
