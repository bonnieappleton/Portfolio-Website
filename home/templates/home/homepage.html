About Me

I am a freelance web developer with four years of experience in the industry. I build beautiful responsive websites for small businesses, charities and NGOs with built in content management systems so that you can have ownership over your own content.

I take on remote work from anywhere in the UK and I am available for Skype calls or where possible I can meet in person. I consider projects with or without designs and where hosting and maintenance are required.

I am co-organiser of codebar Oxford and Django Girls Oxford and I am passionate about promoting diversity in tech. I regularly attend local meetups such as JS Oxford and Oxford Python in order to keep up to date on current technologies and best practices.

Outside of work I am a dressmaker, I read a lot of books (currently on a bit of a graphic novel binge) and I cook a mean bowl of ramen.

If you have a project in mind then I would love to hear from you! You can email me at bemappleton@gmail.com
